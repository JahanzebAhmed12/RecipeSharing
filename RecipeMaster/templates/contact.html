Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a recipe?

Simply create an account, log in, and click "Add Recipe" to share your culinary creations with our community.

Why isn't my recipe showing up?

All recipes go through a moderation process to ensure quality. Your recipe will be visible once approved by our team.

Can I edit my recipes?

Yes! You can edit your own recipes at any time. Edited recipes may need re-approval before being visible to others.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot your password?" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email address.
